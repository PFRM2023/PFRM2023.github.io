Hefei Fengda International Hotel

Fengda International Hotel Hefei is located in the Pearl Square of the Economic and Technological Development Zone, Fuhua Avenue, Hefei City, Anhui Province. It connects the Economic Development Zone, Government Affairs Zone, High-tech Zone, and Binhu New Area. It is 15 minutes away from the high-speed railway station and 45 minutes away from the new airport. It is 300 meters away from the International Convention and Exhibition Center, 400 meters away from Jinzhai Road Viaduct and Jinzhai Road Expressway Exit, with convenient transportation and superior geographical location. The hotel consists of two super-high-rise all-glass curtain wall twin towers with a construction area of more than 120,000 square meters. It has nearly 1,300 guest rooms and 16 luxury banquet halls. It is one of the largest hotels in China in terms of single building scale.
