The third international conference on phase field method and related methods

This is the third international conference on phase field method and related methods to be held in China that involves scientists and researchers in the world.

Topics for this conference include, but are not limited to:

  • 1.Microscopic / Mesoscopic phase-field models
  • 2.Phase field crystal
  • 3.Cellular Automaton
  • 4.Dislocation, crystal plasticity, fracture and fatigue
  • 5.Multiscale modelling and simulations
  • 6.Materials design based on phase field method and related methods
  • 7.Integrated Computational Materials Engineering and Materials genome engineering
  • 8.Demonstration of software and databases for computational thermodynamics, computational kinetics, and phase field simulations
